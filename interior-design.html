Interior Design Magazine

Layouts for Interior Design magazine, a trade publication for architects and interior designers. The magazine features inspiring projects from leading designers as well as innovative products.

Project Walkthrough

Front of the book story walking the reader through the office design of a Los Angeles graphics firm. The story starts with a bold opening image of a close up wall graphic of the owner's baby, the story continues on the subsequent pages to give a broader overview of the office space.

Product Layouts

Some of the most fun and playful layouts are for featured products. Compositions often focus on silhouetted products arranged to show variety within the product line.
