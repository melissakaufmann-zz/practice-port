Flatiron School

Redesign of the Flatiron School’s marketing site. We needed to better incorporate the online web development program and help perspective students make informed decisions about which program is right for them.

Problem

The existing marketing site was heavily focused on the in-person immersive programs and didn’t position the online program equally. We also needed to do a better job of helping students find information that would help them make a decision on what course was right for them.
