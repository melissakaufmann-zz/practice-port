Learn Onboarding

Addition of a progress widget to optimize the onboarding track for Flatiron School’s online learning platform, Learn.co

Problem

Users were dropping off during the onboarding process, especially around the download and installation of a tool called the IDE. A tool that is essential for the platform’s workflow for solving labs.

Discovering the Magazine

We wanted the magazine feature to be easily discoverable. I added a right rail to the existing blogging dashboard with a CTA to the open the magazine. I also added a feed of the most recent student blog posts from the magazine to help pique interest
