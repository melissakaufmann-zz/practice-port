Landing Page Redesign for Marketing Resources

Redesign of our landing pages for downloading marketing content. During the redesign all pages became responsive; forms were moved from right to the left for increased engagement; un-necessary fields in progressive forms were eliminated and buttons became pink for increased visibility.
